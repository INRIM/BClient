INRIM VersICaL Impedance Bridge Balancing Procedure (Non-Shorting)

StepActionActive ChannelDetection portActive Switch ModeCommand
1Set the bridge in the forward configuration
2Adjust E32DHPADHPAAlt+Z
3Adjust E24LPALPAAlt+Z
4Adjust E43DHPBDHPBAlt+Z
5Adjust E55LPA-LPBLPAAlt+Z
6Repeat steps 2-5 until the voltage at LPA and the voltage or the current at DHPB are both nulled
7Save the dataCtrl+S
8Repeat steps 2-7 in the reverse configuration
